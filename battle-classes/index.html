A VERY CLASSY KINGDOM

CLASS SYSTEM OVERVIEW

GAMEPLAY

The Kingdom has 4 class types; Battle Classes, Character Classes, Land Classes and Elemental Classes. Your Battle Class is marked on the top of your character’s NFT (See: Understanding Your Character NFT). You can level up your characters Battle Class through merging in the My Banana Kingdom App (launching in Phase 2).

THE BATTLE CLASSES

ONE FOR EVERY LEVEL OF PLAYER

NOMADIC CLASS

AGILE TRIBES ON THE MOVE

If you’re a Nomadic Class Warrior, Builder, Commander or Oracle you’re able to:

  • Join any Nomadic Tribe in your region.
  • Craft Encampment land structures (for Builders).
  • Join your Chieftain’s Nomadic Tribal War Party.
  • Engage in Free For All combat across all Battle Classes.

As a Nomadic Class Tribal Chieftain you’re an Encampment Plot landowner who can:

  • Have up to of 50 players in your Tribe.
  • Form a War Party with up to 10 members, including yourself, 1 Oracle, 2 Commanders, and 6 Warriors.
  • Use your War Party to battle other Nomadic Tribes.
  • Use your War Party to compete in Tribal Tournaments.
  • Use your War Party to liberate and take over Encampments occupied by Elemental Titans, and the Kong’s in your region.

SETTLED CLASS

IT TAKES A VILLAGE

If you’re a Settled Class Warrior, Builder, Commander or Oracle you’re able to:

  • Join any Nomadic Tribe or Settled Village worldwide.
  • Craft Encampment and Village land structures (for Builders).
  • Join your Chieftain’s Tribal War Party or Village War Party.
  • Engage in Free For All combat with anyone from the Settled Class and up.

As a Settled Class Village Chieftain, you’re a Village Plot landowner who can:

  • Have up to 250 players in your Village.
  • Form a War Party with up to 50 members, including yourself, 1 Oracle, 4 Commanders, and 44 Warriors.
  • Use your War Party to battle other Villages.
  • Use your War Party to compete in Village Tournaments.
  • Use your War Party to liberate and take over Villages occupied by Elemental Titans and the Kong’s, in your region.

ELITE CLASS

HIGH WALLS & HUGE POPULATIONS

If you’re an Elite Class Warrior, Builder, Commander or Oracle you’re able to:

  • Join any Nomadic Tribe, Settled Village or Elite City worldwide.
  • Craft Encampment, Village or City land structures (for Builders).
  • Join your Chieftain’s Tribal War Party, Village War Party or City War Party.
  • Engage in Free For All combat with anyone from the Elite Class and up.

As an Elite Class City Chieftain, you’re a City Plot landowner who can:

  • Have up to 1000 players in your City.
  • Form a War Party with up to 200 members, including yourself, 1 Oracle, 6 Commanders, and 92 Warriors.
  • Use your War Party to battle other Cities.
  • Use your War Party to compete in City Tournaments.
  • Use your War Party to liberate and take over Cities occupied by Elemental Titans and the Kong’s, in your region.

APEX CLASS

WHERE KINGS AND QUEENS ARE FORGED

If you’re an Apex Class Warrior, Builder, Commander or Oracle you’re able to:

  • Join any Nomadic Tribe, Settled Village, Elite City or Apex Kingdom worldwide.
  • Craft Encampment, Village, City and Kingdom land structures (for Builders).
  • Join your Chieftain’s Tribal War Party, Village War Party, City War Party or Kingdom War Party.
  • Engage in Free For All combat with anyone from the Apex Class and up.

As an Apex Class City Chieftain, you’re a Kingdom Plot landowner who can:

  • Have up to 4000 players in your Kingdom.
  • Form a War Party with up to 300 members, including yourself, 1 Oracle, 8 Commanders, and 290 Warriors.
  • Use your War Party to battle other Kingdoms.
  • Use your War Party to compete in Kingdom, Regional & Crown Ascension Tournaments.
  • Use your War Party to liberate and take over entire Kingdoms occupied by Elemental Titans and the Kong’s, in your region.

REGIONAL EMPIRE CLASS

THE NEXT LEVEL FOR APEX KINGDOM'S

How to turn your Apex Class Kingdom into a Regional Empire:

  • Compete monthly within your region for the chance to claim your Region’s Throne.
  • If victorious, you become the King (or Queen) of your region. The Regional Throne is moved to your Kingdom and you are now a Regional Empire.
As a Regional Empire:
  • As the King or Queen of your region, you collect a small tax and choose your Region’s path (Exploration or Warrior), affecting base enhancements for all region players and unlocking advanced enhancements for your Apex Kingdom members.
  • You join an exclusive group of 18 Empires across the Banana Kingdom.
  • Compete against the other 17 Regional Empires in the Crown Ascension Tournament at the Kingsforge Arena.
  • Winning the Crown Ascension Tournament makes you the King (or Queen) of the Banana Kingdom and your Regional Empire becomes the capitol of the Banana Kingdom.

GAME MODES

SINGLE PLAYER

(FREE-FOR-ALL)

PLAYER VS PLAYER (PVP)

Compete in a game-wide Free-For-All against other players in a crusade to earn tokens and secure rare NFTs. Rise through the ranks in your region and face off against the best players from other regions, all vying for monthly prizes and titles.

PLAYER VS ENVIRONMENT (PVE)

Elemental Titans roam the lands, guarding the fragmented remnants of the Kingdom’s Sky Pillars and valuable loot chests. Do you have the skills and courage to overcome the Titans?

MULTIPLAYER

(WAR PARTIES)

PLAYER VS PLAYER (PVP)

Enlist in your Chieftain’s War Party and engage in epic multiplayer battles against other War Parties from your class and region. Tribe vs Tribe, Village vs Village, City vs City, Kingdom vs Kingdom. Who will be the region’s strongest…

PLAYER VS ENVIRONMENT (PVE)

Expand your territory by reclaiming lost Encampments, Villages, Cities, and Kingdoms overrun by Elemental Titans and occupied by the giants of the Banana Kingdom: The Kongs. How far will your empire extend…
