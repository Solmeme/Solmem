MINT A FREE PFP | OPENS MARCH 26TH

By adminFebruary 28, 2024March 4th, 2024No Comments2 min read
WHAT & WHEN

Our first of 5 unique PFP drops. Banana Kingdom Warlords are 1800 1 of 1 unique PFP’S representing the 18 unique regions of the Banana Kingdom — coming to the Arbitrum Nova blockchain on March 26th.

HOW TO GET ONE

Warlords are not for sale, but are free (limit 2 per wallet) for anyone who stakes Banana Coins for Warlord Tokens.

UTILITIES INCLUDED

Warlords will be stakeable when 3X staking opens and remain stakeable for up to 24 months. Warlords will also have varying bonus attack and defense buffs built in when you use them in-game within the region the Warlord is from.

BONUS

Enter for a chance to win one via Premint
