CRAFTING NEW TECHNOLOGY

GAMEPLAY

ELECTRIFYING THE KINGDOM: ADVANCING TECHNOLOGY AND THE STORYLINE

Fragmented Tech plays a crucial role in advancing your kingdom’s technology and storyline. Fragmented Tech comes in two forms:

  1. Fragmented Scrap Tech: These are used to craft new tech items and structural components that are unlocked through the Origin “Tech” and “Blueprint” Scrolls.
  2. Sky Pillar Fragments: These fragments are used to create the Sky Pillar Blocks needed to rebuild the ancient Sky Pillars that are scattered throughout the Kingdom’s 18 Regions.

You can acquire Fragmented Scrap Tech by mining fallen meteorites, and by rebuilding Sky Pillars throughout the game. You can acquire Sky Pillar Fragments by exploring the Kingdom’s 18 Regions.

Once all of the Sky Scroll pieces are found in the Quest for the Origin Scrolls, and all the the Sky Pillars have been rebuilt, it’s time to assemble the Sky Scroll and activate the Pillars to unlock the secrets within, and to begin Part 2 of the Banana Kingdom game.

FIND IT IN GAME
