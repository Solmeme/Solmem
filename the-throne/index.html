18 REGIONAL SEATS OF POWER

GAMEPLAY

..one will rule the Kingdom.

Regional thrones are the Seats of Power and prestige in each of the games 18 unique regions. Claimed by the region's Apex Chieftains and taken by their War Party's in the Apex Tournaments, these Seats of Power are the key to empire building in your Region. As a Regional King, collect a region-wide tax and build out your vision or, pocket the spoils of war. Choose one of the two paths (battle or exploration) for your entire region to activate unique boosts for players across the region — and then prepare yourself.

Because only one will rule the Kingdom.

The one left standing when the Kingdom's 18 Regional Powerhouse Chieftains collide at the Kingsforge Arena — where Kings aren't made, they're forged in the embers of the Crown Ascension Tournament.

Will you be ready?

— Background Photo: Regional Throne of the Silver Isles
