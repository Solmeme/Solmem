CHARACTER CLASSES

WARRIOR CLASS

MERGE 5 WARRIORS OF THE SAME CLASS TO LEVEL YOUR CHARACTER UP TO THE NEXT BATTLE CLASS

Warriors are the only class that can take on the Kingdoms largest class of player in Free-For-All battle: The Adventurer – the Kingdom’s free-to-play players who are on The Path of the Warrior.

Join a Tribe, Village, City or Kingdom and get accepted into their War Party and you’ll be paired up a Commander who will give you access to powerful weapons and gear through the War Party armory.

BUILDER CLASS

MERGE 5 BUILDERS OF THE SAME CLASS TO LEVEL YOUR CHARACTER UP TO THE NEXT BATTLE CLASS

All Builders are part of the the Banana Kingdom’s Builders Guild right out of the gate. Only builders can craft and sell the many structural NFTs that will see the Kingdom take shape.
This includes, homes, buildings, defense/perimeter walls, architectural items and more.
Builders don’t just tinker all day however, they’re fighters, know to wield a variety of massive warhammers.

All Builders are automatic members of the Banana Kingdom’s Builders Guild. Builders have the exclusive rights to craft and sell structural NFTs, which are essential to developing the entire Banana Kingdom. These structures includes homes, buildings, defense walls, architectural items, and more.

Builders may focus mainly on their craft, but they’re solely focused on the Kingdom’s construction; they’re also formidable opponents known to wield a variety of massive warhammers, with precision.

COMMANDER CLASS

MERGE 5 COMMANDERS OF THE SAME CLASS TO LEVEL YOUR CHARACTER UP TO THE NEXT BATTLE CLASS

Commanders have a unique place in a War Party. Scouting talent is their hobby because they know they’ll earn from it.

Commanders can stockpile a War Party’s armory and lend weapons and gear to the party’s warriors, earning a percentage of Tokens the Warriors make from having their gear equipped. What’s more is if a Tournament or a raid comes and a few warriors can’t make it, a Commander can takeover gameplay for those Warriors and earn half of what the Warriors would have gotten if the tournament or raid is successful is.

ORACLE CLASS

MERGE 5 ORACLES OF THE SAME CLASS TO LEVEL YOUR CHARACTER UP TO THE NEXT BATTLE CLASS

Oracles are extremely rare in that only one is allowed on any single plot of land, no matter how large the plot. Oracles are the master’s of magic, sole crafters the Kingdom’s elemental shards and the branders of glyphs. All Oracles are part of the Oracle’s Guild and retain exclusive rights to glyph branding and the crafting of elemental shards (both of which enhance the majority of crafted items in the Kingdom).

CHIEFTAIN CLASS

MERGE 5 CHIEFTAINS OF THE SAME CLASS TO LEVEL UP YOUR CHARACTER, AND YOUR LAND TO THE NEXT CLASS

Chieftains are the landowners of the Kingdom. Overseeing the development of their plots, and the strategic takeovers of open plots and warfare against rival War Parties.

Chieftains earn a cut of many things that happen on their land and get the final say on who can live within their territorial borders.

Only a Chieftain can take a Regional Throne, and only a Chieftain can become King (or Queen) of the Banana Kingdom.
